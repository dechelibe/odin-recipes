Lasagna

Description

This homemade lasagna is a rich, comforting Italian dish made with layers of tender pasta, savory meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and melted mozzarella. Perfect for family dinners or special occasions, this dish is sure to be a crowd-pleaser.

Ingredients

  • For the Meat Sauce

    • 1 pound (450g) ground beef (or Italian sausage)
    • 1 small onion, finely chopped
    • 2 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 can (28 oz / 800g) crushed tomatoes
    • 1 can (15 oz / 425g) tomato sauce
    • 1 can (6 oz / 170g) tomato paste
    • ½ cup (120ml) water
    • 1 teaspoon salt
    • 1 teaspoon sugar
    • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
    • 1 teaspoon dried basil
    • ½ teaspoon black pepper

  • For the Ricotta Mixture

    • 1 container (15 oz / 425g) ricotta cheese
    • 1 egg
    • ¼ cup (25g) grated Parmesan cheese
    • 1 teaspoon dried parsley

  • Assembling the Lasagna

    • 12 lasagna noodles (regular or oven-ready)
    • 2 cups (225g) shredded mozzarella cheese
    • ¼ cup (25g) grated Parmesan cheese (for topping)

Steps

  • Prepare the Meat Sauce

    1. In a large skillet or saucepan, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
    2. Add chopped onions and garlic, cooking until softened (about 3 minutes).
    3. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water.
    4. Season with salt, sugar, oregano, basil, and black pepper.
    5. Simmer the sauce on low heat for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.

  • Cook the Noodles

    1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
    2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
    3. If using oven-ready noodles, skip this step.

  • Prepare the Ricotta Mixture

    • In a medium bowl, mix the ricotta cheese, egg, Parmesan cheese, and parsley until well combined.

  • Assemble the Lasagna

    1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
    2. Spread 1 cup of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch (23x33cm) baking dish.
    3. Layer 3 cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce.
    4. Spread ⅓ of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
    5. Sprinkle with ½ cup mozzarella cheese.
    6. Repeat layers: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella until you reach the top.
    7. Finish with a final layer of noodles, meat sauce, and the remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.

  • Bake the Lasagna

    1. Cover the dish with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
    2. Remove the foil and bake for another 15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
    3. Let the lasagna rest for 10-15 minutes before slicing to allow the layers to set.
