Pizza

Description

This homemade pizza recipe creates a delicious, crispy, and chewy crust topped with rich tomato sauce, gooey melted cheese, and your favorite toppings. Perfect for a fun family dinner or a casual get-together, this pizza is easy to make and can be customized with your favorite flavors.

Ingredients

For the Dough

  • ½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour (or bread flour for a chewier crust)
  • teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup (180ml) warm water (110°F/43°C)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil

For the Sauce

  • cup (240ml) canned crushed tomatoes
  • 1 teaspoon olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • ½ teaspoon dried oregano
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon sugar

For the Toppings

  • 1 ½ cups (170g) shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ¼ cup (25g) grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • Favorite toppings: pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, olives, onions, or fresh basil

Steps

Prepare the Dough

  1. In a small bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until foamy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour, mixing until a rough dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl, cover with a towel, and let it rise in a warm place for 1 hour or until doubled in size.

Make the Sauce

  1. In a small saucepan, heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the crushed tomatoes, salt, oregano, garlic powder, and sugar.
  3. Simmer for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. Remove from heat and let cool.

Assemble the Pizza

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat up.
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Spread a thin layer of sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges.
  5. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  6. Add your favorite toppings.

Bake the Pizza

  1. Place the pizza in the preheated oven and bake for 10-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
  2. Remove from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes before slicing.
  3. Garnish with fresh basil or extra Parmesan cheese if desired.
